# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

Swapp respects your privacy.

## Information Collection

Swapp does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app does not require:

- account creation
- login
- email address
- phone number

Swapp does not use:

- analytics services
- advertising SDKs
- tracking technologies
- third-party data collection

## Local Data Storage

Sticker collection progress and app data are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Swapp does not use third-party services that collect personal information.

## Children's Privacy

Swapp does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

## Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can remove all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the app. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

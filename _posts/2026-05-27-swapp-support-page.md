# Swapp Support

Thank you for using Swapp.

Swapp is a simple and native sticker album tracker designed for iPhone and iPad.

## Need Help?

If you experience issues, have suggestions, or want to report a bug, feel free to contact:

[eacardenase@gmail.com](mailto:eacardenase@gmail.com)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Swapp require an account?

No. Swapp works completely offline and does not require registration.

### Is my data synced online?

No. Your collection data is stored locally on your device.

### How do I reset my collection?

You can remove all app data by deleting and reinstalling the app.

### Is Swapp affiliated with FIFA or Panini?

No. Swapp is an independent companion app and is not affiliated with FIFA or Panini.
